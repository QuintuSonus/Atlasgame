How to Play

✦ ✦ ✦

1. A satellite image of a mystery location is shown — zoomed in tight.

2. Guess the country it belongs to. You get 5 attempts.

3. After each wrong guess, the camera pulls back to reveal more context, and you'll see the distance and direction from your guess to the target.

4. Find the country before the camera reaches space — or before you run out of guesses.
